About fest

Welcome to the Tesseract 2019

Tesseract is PDPU's Annual Science and Technical Festival. Every year, students from all walks of the Unversity come together to celebrate aspirations, creativity and innovation in their own unique ways. The three-day festival entails everything, from gaming to making robots, from lectures and workshops to a glamorous EDM night.
